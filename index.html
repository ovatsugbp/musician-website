Concept

Jane Doe is an English singer and primary songwriter. Her debut studio album, Jane Doe, was released in 2019; on 17 January, the album reached the top position on the UK Albums Chart after being on the chart for 28 consecutive weeks. Doe won the BRIT Award for Best British Album in 2019. The singer's second single, Ceremonials, released in October 2019, debuted at number one in the UK and number six on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart. The band's third single, Blue and Beautiful, was released in February 2020 to positive reviews from music critics and topped the UK and US singles chart.,

In 2018, Doe released a book titled Useless Magic, a collection of lyrics and poems written by her, along with illustrations.
